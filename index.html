    Behavior Therapy

    Behavior Therapy is a community based program that offers intensive counseling and intervention service where your child may need it most.Behavior Therapy can provide home interventions, school interventions, and community intervention at time of service. Service are counseling and intervention using Cognitive Behavior Therapy Modality which is the standard for community based services in Virginia.Therapy can be offered for up to 20 hours a week,but hours of service are subject to approval by insurance providers.

    Intensive In Home Therapy

    Intensive In Home Therapy is a community based program that offers intensive counseling and intervention service provides at home intervention at time of service. Service are counseling and intervention using Cognitive Behavior Therapy Modality which is the standard for community based services in Virginia.Therapy can be offered for up to 16 hours a week,but hours of service are subject to approval by insurance providers.

    Therapeutic Day Treatment

    Therapeutic Day Treatment is a community based program that offers intensive counseling in school,alternative to school, or in an after school Therapy. Service are counseling and intervention using Cognitive Behavior Therapy Modality which is the standard for community based services in Virginia.Therapy can be offered for up to 18 hours a week,but hours of service are subject to approval by insurance providers
